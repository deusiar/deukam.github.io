Project Management Skills Using Jira - Resulam

Leading a Team for Audio Phrasebooks in African Languages

Overview

successfully led a team of 10 people at Resulam to develop audio phrasebooks for African languages. The project spanned two years and involved the use of Jira for task management and collaboration.

Project Objectives

  • Project Ideation
  • Project Planning
  • Writing books in the base language Fe'efe'e
  • Finding translators for various African languages
  • Explaining the project scope
  • Following up on tasks
  • Planning for audio recording
  • Searching for studios for recording
  • Finding suitable readers
  • Overseeing audio quality checks
  • Building a website to host the phrasebooks

Project Phases

  • Ideation: Conceptualized the project and defined its objectives.
  • Planning: Developed a comprehensive roadmap using Jira to assign tasks and track progress.
  • Implementation:
    • Books were written in Fe'efe'e as the base language.
    • Translators were recruited to expand into multiple African languages.
    • Studios and readers were identified for high-quality recordings.
  • Recording: Organized studio sessions and followed up on audio quality.
  • Finalization: Ensured the phrasebooks were published online through a custom website.

Key Achievements

  • Successfully managed a diverse, multilingual team.
  • Ensured timely completion of tasks and deliverables over two years.
  • Produced high-quality audio phrasebooks in multiple African languages.
  • Developed an accessible website for distributing the phrasebooks.
